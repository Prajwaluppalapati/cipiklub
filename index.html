About CIPIKlub

What is CIPIKlub?
CIPIKlub (Create, invent, programme, innovate) is a student-led coding club which is registered under hack club that brings young makers and coders together to learn, build, and share technology projects. We build Apps, websites, games and hardware projects together in a fun and collaborative environment.

We also conduct workshops, hackathons, and coding challenges to help members improve their coding skills and explore technology.

Our mission and vision
At CIPIKlub, our mission is to empower students to think boldly, build fearlessly, and innovate responsibly. We believe that the best way to learn is by doing — experimenting, failing, improving, and ultimately creating something that matters. Our vision is to nurture a space where curiosity thrives, ideas turn into real-world projects, and every student feels confident exploring technology regardless of their skill level. CIPIKlub aims to become a launchpad for young innovators, helping them develop technical skills, leadership qualities, and a mindset for lifelong learning.

Whether you’re curious about programming and robotics, CIPIKlub is a friendly place to try things hands-on, ask questions, and work with others CIPIKlubbers.
